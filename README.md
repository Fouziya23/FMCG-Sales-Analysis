# FMCG Sales Analysis

End-to-end analysis of 190,000+ FMCG sales records across brands, regions and channels from 2022 to 2024.


## Tools Used
- **Python** — Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
- **Power BI** — Interactive sales dashboard

---

## Dataset
- 190,757 rows across 14 columns
- Fields: Date, SKU, Brand, Category, Region, Channel, Pack Type, Price, Promotion Flag, Units Sold

---

## Key Business Questions Answered

| Question | Finding |

| Total units sold? | 3.8M |
| Sales distribution across regions? | Equal — PL-North, PL-South, PL-Central all ~1.26M |
| Top brand? | SnBrand2 — 543K units |
| Best channel? | E-commerce — 1.26M units |
| Does promotion impact sales? | Yes — promoted SKUs outperform non-promoted |
| Data period? | 2022 — 2024 |

---

## Analysis Highlights

**Python EDA**
- Units sold distribution by region, brand, channel, category
- Yearly sales trend 2022-2024
- Promotion impact analysis
- Top 10 brands by units sold

**Power BI Dashboard**
- Interactive brand and region filters
- Sales revenue by date and region
- Sales over time trend
- Sales by channel breakdown
- KPI cards — Total Revenue, Avg Revenue, Units Sold

---

## Key Insights
- All three regions perform equally — no single region dominates
- E-commerce, Retail and Discount channels are almost equally distributed
- Promotions positively impact units sold
- SnBrand2 leads significantly over other brands
